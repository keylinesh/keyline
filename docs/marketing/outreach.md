# Founder Outreach Kit

The first paid teams come from direct conversations, not channels. Target:
tech leads at 2 to 10 person agencies and startups. The conversion event is
a second member joining a workspace.

## The list (founder homework)

50 companies: Clutch.co (Bulgaria, Balkans, EU agencies), LinkedIn contacts,
past colleagues, local dev communities. Track in a plain sheet: company,
person, channel, date, reply, outcome.

## First message (EN)

> Hey {name}. Quick question from a fellow dev: how does your team share
> .env files when a new person joins a project?
>
> I ask because it kept biting me (Slack DMs, stale copies, no revoke), so
> I built Keyline: push a .env encrypted with one command, teammates pull
> it, revoke in one command when someone leaves. Two minutes to set up,
> $19 flat for the whole team.
>
> If sharing secrets annoys you too, I'd love your honest take. And if it
> doesn't stick with your team, that's exactly the feedback I need.

## First message (BG)

> Здравей, {name}. Бърз въпрос от колега разработчик: как вашият екип
> споделя .env файлове, когато нов човек влезе в проект?
>
> Питам, защото на мен това постоянно ми създаваше проблеми (Slack

> напускане режеш достъпа с една команда. Настройва се за две минути,
> $19 фиксирано за целия екип.
>
> Ако и на вас това ви дразни, ще се радвам на честно мнение. А ако не
> се задържи при вас, точно това ми е нужната обратна връзка.

## Follow-up (one only, 4 days later)

> No worries if this isn't a pain for you. One-liner version: encrypted
> .env sharing, two minutes, keyline.sh. If it's ever useful, it's there.

## Objections

- "We use 1Password/vault already": "Great, honestly. Keyline only wins
  when you want .env-native and per-environment access without the setup.
  If your flow works, keep it."
- "Is it secure?": "Encrypted on your laptop before upload; we hold
  ciphertext we cannot read. The design is public: keyline.sh/security.
  Don't trust me, read it."
- "$19 for what Slack does free": "Slack keeps every secret forever with
  no revoke. The $19 is for the moment someone leaves the team."
- Price hesitation: offer the first month free ("tell me it didn't stick
  and I'll refund, no questions" is already our refund policy).

## Cadence

10 personal messages per week. Expect: 10 sent, 4 replies, 2 trials, 1
paying team per 2 weeks at first. Every reply gets logged as beta-feedback
if it names a stumble.
