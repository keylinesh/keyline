# Keyline — Voice & Copy

Keep it short. Write like a developer talking to a developer.

## Rules

- Short sentences. One idea each.
- No em-dash "—" as a connector. Use a period or comma. Split into two sentences.
- Plain words over clever ones.
- Say what it does, not how amazing it is.

## Examples

| Don't | Do |
|---|---|
| You're on the list — we'll be in touch. ✓ | You're on the list ✓ |
| Something went wrong — please try again. | Something went wrong. Try again. |
| Drop your email and we'll let you know the moment it's ready — no spam, just the launch. | Drop your email. We'll tell you when it's ready. No spam. |

Applies everywhere: UI strings, buttons, modals, docs, error messages.
